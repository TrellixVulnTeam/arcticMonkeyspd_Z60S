About The Band

Arctic Monkeys are an English rock band formed in Sheffield in 2002. The group consists of Alex Turner (lead vocals, guitar, keyboards), Jamie Cook (guitar, keyboards), Nick O'Malley (bass guitar, backing vocals), and Matt Helders (drums, backing vocals).

Band Members

  • Alex Turner: lead vocals, lead guitar (2002–present), keyboards (2006–2009, 2017–2019)
  • Jamie Cook – rhythm guitar (2002–present), keyboards (2017–present), backing vocals (2002–2007)
  • Matt Helders – drums, percussion, backing and occasional lead vocals (2002–present)
  • Nick O'Malley – bass guitar, backing vocals (2006–present)
